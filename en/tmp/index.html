Thin Metal Plates

  • 2023.01

These are examples of thin metal plates made of steel that are easy to obtain and react to magnets. It is recommended to use plates 0.3 mm or thicker, as thinner plates may have weak magnetic response.
Plate 1 can be obtained in large quantities and is suitable for assembling many paper models. Plates 2 and 3 are recommended for assembling only one or two paper models.
